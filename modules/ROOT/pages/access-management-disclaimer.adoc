= Configuring a Disclaimer for Anypoint Platform PCE
ifndef::env-site,env-github[]
include::_attributes.adoc[]
endif::[]

To configure a disclaimer for your Anypoint Platform Private Cloud Edition (Anypoint Platform PCE), you can add a custom message to your login page that users in your organization see every time they log in:

. From Anypoint Platform, select *Access Management* > *Disclaimer*.
. Enter your disclaimer text (you can also set up a custom HTML message).
. Select *Save*.
