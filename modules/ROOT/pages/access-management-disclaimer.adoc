ifndef::env-site,env-github[]
include::_attributes.adoc[]
endif::[]

You can add a custom message to your login page that users in your organization see every time they log in:

. From Anypoint Platform, select *Access Management* > *Disclaimer*.
. Enter your disclaimer text.
. Select *Save*.

image::on-prem-features-d22af.png[]

You can also set up a custom HTML message.
